  Sélectionner FR (France)   ──→  Affiche chaînes FRANÇAISES 🇫🇷

HOW IT WORKS (Flux Complet)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  1. User clicks ⚙️ Settings button
       ↓
  2. Settings modal opens
       ↓
  3. User clicks "GB United Kingdom"
       ↓
  4. Settings.vue emits: countryChanged("UK")
       ↓
  5. App.vue receives event → onCountryChanged("UK")
       ↓
  6. selectedCountry.value = "UK" (Vue.js reactive)
       ↓
  7. watch() detects change → TRIGGERS AUTOMATICALLY
       ↓
  8. loadPlaylistForMode("home") executes
       ↓
  9. getPlaylistUrl("UK", "home") returns:
     https://iptv-org.github.io/iptv/countries/gb.m3u
       ↓
  10. loadPlaylist(url, "home") fetches data
       ↓
  11. tvs.value = parsed (Template reactive update)
       ↓
  12. selectFirstChannel() loads first UK channel
       ↓
  13. Nav flag updates: 🇫🇷 → 🇬🇧
       ↓
  14. DONE! Homepage displays UK channels instantly
